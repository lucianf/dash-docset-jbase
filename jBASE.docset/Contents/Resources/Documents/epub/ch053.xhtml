BREAK ON/OFF

Enables or disables the BREAK key. In UNIX terms, the BREAK key is known more commonly as the interrupt sequence intr defined by the stty command.

COMMAND SYNTAX

BREAK ON / BREAK OFF

EXAMPLE

   BREAK OFF
   CRT "Next 5 seconds Ctrl-C makes no action"
   MSLEEP(5000)
   BREAK ON
   CRT "And next 5 seconds Ctrl-C invokes debugger"
   MSLEEP(5000)

Output (user is to try to press Ctrl-C at both prompts):

    Next 5 seconds Ctrl-C makes no action
    And next 5 seconds Ctrl-C invokes debugger
    Interrupt signal
    Source changed to .\test2.b
    0009    MSLEEP(5000)
    jBASE debugger->
